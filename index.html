FAQs on Hair Loss Treatment

What Is The Cost Of This Treatment? +

The cost of hair loss treatment will depend on the type of hair loss you are facing and the severity of the condition. After evaluating this, your doctor may decide the modality of treatment based on which the cost may vary. For more information on this, you may contact us.

How Many Sessions Of Hair Loss Treatment Do You Need To See Results? +

Doctors recommend a course of 6-8 monthly sessions to tackle early hereditary hair loss. Depending on your individual case, they may recommend follow-up sessions to maintain the results.

Is Hair Loss Treatment A Permanent Treatment? +

Thoya's hair loss treatment offers long-lasting results that are sustainable with minimal follow-up sessions and post-care when combined with oral and topical medications.

Who Is An Ideal Candidate For Hair Treatment? +

An ideal candidate for hair treatment is someone who has small areas of alopecia areata or someone who is dealing with constant hair fall/thinning. To undergo the treatment, our dermatologist will perform a few investigations to know the root cause in detail before proceeding with the treatment.

Are There Any Side Effects? +

No, there are no significant side-effects as Thoya's Hair Loss Treatment is 100% safe. However, some clients may experience transient discomfort or heaviness of the head. Your dermatologist may prescribe analgesic to alleviate your discomfort if any.

Is It A Painful Procedure? +

Hair Regrowth Treatment at Thoya is a non-surgical treatment that involves no cuts, blood loss and pain. However, one may experience minimal discomfort, which is bearable.

Is There A Recovery Period Or Downtime For The Treatment? +

Fortunately, there is no recovery period or downtime after the treatment. A patient is only advised to not wash the head for 12 to 24 hours after the procedure.
